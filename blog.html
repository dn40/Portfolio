DOUGLAS NEVILLE

A gambling man taking a chance that he will actually learn how to develop anything more than a headache. Here he shares his insight into the world of front-end development. The dark underbelly of a world littered with the corpses of those less committed!

BLOG of a Sleep Deprived Developer

I sit here quietly. Chipping away at a seemingly impossible assignment but the distractions are building up. Vanessa is right next to me. She knows the answers but won't say peep. Jerry is sucking up to Mike again. Sickening. Steve took his shoes off. I'm sure thats good for everyone. Phuong is typing so fast it sounds like she is beating her computer. Dan is just staring at his computer. SAY SOMETHING! How am I supposed to get anything done???

Energy Drinks

If you aren't taking in enough caffiene to kill a horse then you are going to pass out. Late nights battling programs where success hinges on a single missing semicolon! Drink up.

My Hands Hurt

I'm hardly ever consistantly typing but my wrists are locked in the ready position. Ready for action once a thought, a breakthrough, an idea comes to light. Poised and ready to defeat bugs that I may or may not have caused.

Bloodshot Eyeballs

You try staring at a computer screen for 16hrs a day. Where's my glasses? There will be a point where everything blends together. It will look almost like heiroglyphics. Hang in there. Stand up and walk around a bit to get the blood flowing. Vision should return within 3-5 minutes. Its overrated anyways. Some of my best work came to me in a dream.
